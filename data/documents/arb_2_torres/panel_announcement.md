### About the Moderator

Judge Torres served as a United States District Judge for the Northern District of California from 2004 until his retirement, and now maintains a full-time commercial arbitration and mediation practice based in San Francisco. He is a Fellow of the College of Commercial Arbitrators and a member of the AAA Commercial Panel. He is admitted to practice in California, the District of Columbia, and New York.

### Session Description

This panel will explore the increasingly common career arc from federal judicial service into private commercial dispute resolution, with a focus on how former judges adapt their decision-making style to the arbitral context, manage party expectations, and navigate market dynamics in a crowded neutrals market.

CLE credit: 1.5 hours, General (California).
