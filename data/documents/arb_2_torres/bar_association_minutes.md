# California Lawyers Association — ADR Section

**Executive Committee Meeting Minutes** | November 14, 2023

**Location:** Zoom (video conference)
**Time called to order:** 4:03 PM Pacific

## Attendance

A quorum was present. The chair, Vice-Chair, Treasurer, and seven of the nine regional representatives joined the call. Two regional reps had given prior notice of absence.

## Approval of October Minutes

October minutes were circulated by email on November 7. No corrections were noted. Approved by unanimous voice vote.

## Treasurer's Report

Section dues collections for FY2023 are on track at 94% of budget. The reserve fund balance stands at approximately $172,000. The treasurer recommended no changes to dues for 2024; the committee accepted the recommendation.

## Upcoming Programs

The annual neutral-skills training is confirmed for March 8–9, 2024, at the Long Beach Convention Center. Registration opens December 1. CLE credit applications are in progress.

## Adjournment

Meeting adjourned at 4:51 PM Pacific. Next meeting: December 12.
