# Featured Listing — 23 Cedar Ridge Lane, Greenwich, CT

**Listing Agent:** James Whitfield, Sotheby's International Realty — Greenwich Office

Beautifully maintained 1928 Tudor on a quiet cul-de-sac in mid-Greenwich. Six bedrooms, five and a half baths, approximately 5,400 square feet on 1.2 acres. Original leaded-glass windows, restored slate roof (2019), updated kitchen with Wolf range and dual Sub-Zero refrigerators, walk-up attic. Detached two-car carriage house with finished studio above. Heated saltwater pool installed 2017. Town water, town sewer, generator. Convenient to downtown, train, and the Round Hill Country Club.

**Offered at:** $4,295,000
**Property tax (2023):** $42,118

For private showings contact James Whitfield directly. James joined Sotheby's Greenwich office in 2014 and is consistently among the top ten producing agents in lower Fairfield County. He lives in Riverside with his wife and three children.
