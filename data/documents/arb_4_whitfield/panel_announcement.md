# SIFMA Compliance & Legal Annual Seminar 2024

## Featured Panel Announcement

**"The Arbitrator's View: What Neutrals Are Seeing in Today's Securities Docket"**

*Tuesday, March 19, 2024 | 2:15 PM – 3:30 PM ET*
*Marriott Marquis, Broadway Ballroom | New York, NY*

---

This year's perennially popular "View from the Bench" session returns with a refreshed lineup of leading FINRA and AAA neutrals offering candid perspectives on hearing dynamics, evolving claim types, and what counsel can do to put their best case forward.

**Panelists:**

- **James R. Whitfield, Esq.** — Principal, Whitfield ADR Services LLC (New York)
- Hon. Marisol Vega (Ret.) — JAMS (Los Angeles)
- Dean P. Okafor, Esq. — Okafor Neutral Services (Chicago)

**Moderator:** Rachel Kim, Esq., Associate General Counsel, SIFMA

---

**About James R. Whitfield, Esq.**

Jim Whitfield is a full-time arbitrator and mediator based in New York. A former partner at Hartwell & Brennan LLP, he sits on the FINRA Chairperson-Eligible roster and the AAA Commercial and Large, Complex Case panels. He has chaired or sat as sole arbitrator in over 140 commercial matters since 2018, with a focus on securities, broker-dealer, and post-closing M&A disputes.
