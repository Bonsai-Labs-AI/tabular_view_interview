# FINRA — Regulatory Notice 23-12

**Date:** September 2023
**Subject:** Reminder regarding electronic discovery obligations in arbitration

## Background

FINRA is reminding member firms of their obligations under the FINRA Code of Arbitration Procedure with respect to the production of electronically stored information (ESI) in customer and intra-industry arbitrations. The Office of Dispute Resolution Services continues to see disputes in which respondents have not preserved relevant ESI in a manner consistent with the Code and applicable guidance.

## Key Reminders

Member firms are reminded that:

1. Litigation hold obligations attach upon reasonable anticipation of a claim, not only upon receipt of a Statement of Claim;
2. Production of ESI in native format may be ordered where reasonably accessible;
3. The Director of Arbitration may impose sanctions, including adverse-inference instructions, in cases of failure to preserve.

## Questions

Member firms with questions regarding this Notice should contact FINRA's Office of Dispute Resolution Services at the contacts listed on FINRA's website.

*— Issued by the FINRA Office of Dispute Resolution Services.*
