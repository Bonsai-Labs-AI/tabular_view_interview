# AIPLA 2024 Annual Meeting — Featured Panel Announcement

## Resolving Cross-Border Semiconductor Patent Disputes: Arbitration as the New Forum

**Date:** Thursday, October 24, 2024
**Time:** 2:15 PM – 3:45 PM ET
**Venue:** Marriott Marquis Washington, DC — Salon 2/3

The American Intellectual Property Law Association is pleased to announce a featured panel exploring the rapid growth of international arbitration as a venue for high-stakes semiconductor and standards-essential patent disputes. With chip-sector tensions across the U.S., Japan, Korea, and Taiwan driving record dispute volume, parties are increasingly turning to AAA-ICDR, JCAA, and SIAC over national courts.

**Featured Speakers:**

- **Hon. Margaret L. Reilly (ret.)** — JAMS, San Francisco
- **Dr. Yuki Tanaka** — Independent Arbitrator, Palo Alto / Tokyo
- **Kenji Morimoto** — General Counsel, Sakai Semiconductor Holdings
- **Prof. David A. Lichtman** — University of Chicago Law School (moderator)

**About Dr. Tanaka:** Yuki Tanaka is an independent arbitrator focused on cross-border IP and technology disputes. She sits on the panels of the JCAA, AAA-ICDR, and SIAC, and serves as adjunct professor of law at Keio University. She holds a Ph.D. from Stanford Law School and is admitted in California and as a Japanese bengoshi.

CLE credit pending in NY, CA, VA, and IL. Open to all registered AIPLA Annual Meeting attendees.
