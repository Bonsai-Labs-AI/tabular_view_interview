## Arbitration Practice

Dr. Okonkwo accepts appointments as sole, presiding, and party-appointed arbitrator under ICC, LCIA, UNCITRAL, ICSID, and CRCICA rules. Her docket focuses on cross-border commercial disputes, investor-state matters, and infrastructure and energy projects across West Africa, the Gulf, and Lusophone markets.

**Primary subject-matter areas:**

- Investor-state arbitration (BIT and ECT claims)
- Cross-border infrastructure and concession disputes
- Energy (upstream and midstream) and mining
- Construction (FIDIC-form) disputes
