# Nigerian Bar Association — Section on Business Law

**Quarterly Bulletin** | Q3 2019

## New Arbitration and Mediation Bill — Update

The Federal Ministry of Justice's working group on revisions to the 1988 Arbitration and Conciliation Act circulated a fresh draft this quarter. The draft incorporates several recommendations from the Lagos Court of Arbitration and is expected to be tabled at the next sitting of the National Assembly. Key proposed changes include express recognition of interim measures and a clearer framework for emergency arbitrators.

## CPD Programmes

Three continuing professional development programmes are scheduled for the upcoming quarter, in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt respectively. Topics include construction dispute resolution, the enforcement of foreign awards, and recent developments in admiralty arbitration. Registration details are available on the section's portal.

## In Memoriam

The section notes with sadness the passing of Chief Olufemi Akinwumi, SAN, a foundational member of the section, in August.
