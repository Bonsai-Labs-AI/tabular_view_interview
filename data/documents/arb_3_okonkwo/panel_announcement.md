# GAR Live Lagos 2024 — Panel Announcement

**Panel III: "Concessions, Stabilisation, and the State — Drafting Around the Next Decade of African Infrastructure Disputes"**

Friday, 18 October 2024 | 14:30–16:00 WAT
The Civic Centre, Ozumba Mbadiwe Avenue, Victoria Island, Lagos

---

We are delighted to confirm that **Dr. Amara Okonkwo** will speak on Panel III at GAR Live Lagos 2024.

Dr. Okonkwo is an independent arbitrator based between London and Lagos. She sits regularly under ICC, LCIA, and UNCITRAL rules in cross-border commercial and investor-state disputes, with a particular focus on African infrastructure and energy projects. She holds a doctorate in international law from the University of Geneva and is a member of the LCIA Court and the ICC's Africa Commission. She speaks English, French, and Igbo.

She will be joined on the panel by Mariama Diallo (Three Crowns, Paris), Adebayo Ogunlesi Jr. (Templars, Lagos), and Prof. Kwame Boateng (University of Ghana, Legon). The panel will be moderated by Funke Adekoya SAE, of AELEX.

Registration is open to GAR subscribers and invited guests. A drinks reception in the rooftop garden will follow from 16:30.

*GAR Live Lagos 2024 is supported by the Lagos Court of Arbitration and the African Arbitration Association.*
