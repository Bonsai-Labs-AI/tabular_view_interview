## Arbitration Practice

Prof. Vance maintains an active arbitration practice through Vance Arbitration Chambers, sitting as sole arbitrator, chair, and party-appointed arbitrator in commercial, post-M&A, and energy-sector disputes. She accepts appointments under ICC, AAA-ICDR, LCIA, SIAC, and UNCITRAL rules.

**Primary subject-matter areas:**
- Post-acquisition indemnification and earn-out disputes
- Joint venture and shareholder disputes
- Energy and infrastructure (upstream oil & gas, LNG)
- Reinsurance and complex commercial contracts
