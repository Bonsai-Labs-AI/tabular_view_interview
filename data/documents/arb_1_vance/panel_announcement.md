# NYIAC Spring Forum 2024

## "The Reasoned Award: Drafting for Enforcement and Survival on Review"

**Date:** 17 April 2024
**Time:** 2:00 PM – 4:30 PM (reception to follow)
**Venue:** New York International Arbitration Center, 150 East 42nd Street, 17th Floor, New York

The New York International Arbitration Center is pleased to announce the spring installment of its Practitioners' Forum series, bringing together leading arbitrators, counsel, and in-house users to discuss the craft of award drafting and the standards applied by supervisory courts in New York, Paris, and London.

**Featured Panelists:**

- **Prof. Eleanor M. Vance**, Vance Arbitration Chambers (New York)
- **Hon. Faith N. Okello (ret.)**, Independent Arbitrator (London)
- **Carlos Reyes-Marín**, Partner, Reyes Marín & Asociados (Madrid)
- **Moderator:** Prof. Rajiv Patel, NYU School of Law

**About Prof. Vance:** Eleanor Vance is a full-time independent arbitrator based in New York and a Charles Evans Hughes Professor of Law emerita at Columbia Law School. She has sat in more than 90 commercial arbitrations under the principal institutional rules. She is fluent in English and French and is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators.

CLE credit (2.0 hours, areas of professional practice) is available for New York-admitted attorneys. Registration is complimentary for NYIAC members and $75 for non-members. Space is limited.
