# New York City Bar Association

## 2023 Continuing Legal Education Program — Spring/Summer Catalog

### Program Brochure

---

The New York City Bar Association is pleased to announce its Spring/Summer 2023 Continuing Legal Education programming. The catalog that follows presents the Association's full slate of accredited CLE offerings for the period February through August 2023. All programs are accredited for New York Transitional and Non-Transitional CLE credit unless otherwise noted, and credit details for New Jersey, Connecticut, California, and Pennsylvania are provided per program.

Programs are offered in three delivery formats: in-person at the City Bar House at 42 West 44th Street; live-streamed (with full Q&A participation); and on-demand recorded. Members of the City Bar receive a 30 percent discount on registration fees; members of City Bar committees receive an additional 10 percent discount on programs sponsored by their committee.

For registration, please visit cle.nycbar.org or call (212) 382-6600.

#### **Negotiating and Drafting the Modern M&A Earn-Out: A Practical Workshop**

*Friday, February 24, 2023 | 9:00 a.m. – 1:00 p.m. EST | In-person and live-streamed*
*4.0 NY CLE credits (3.5 Professional Practice, 0.5 Ethics)*

This intensive half-day workshop will guide participants through the principal commercial, drafting, and dispute-resolution issues that arise in the negotiation of earn-out provisions in middle-market and large-cap transactions, with particular attention to recent Delaware Chancery developments and to the management of post-closing accounting disputes.

- Sasha Vinogradov, Of Counsel, Reinhart, Mullen & Boyd LLP

### Registration and Fees

All registration is handled through the City Bar's CLE registration portal at cle.nycbar.org. Standard registration fees are as follows:

| Program length | Member | Non-member |
|---|---|---|
| Half-day (3–4 credits) | $295 | $445 |
| Full-day (6–8 credits) | $495 | $745 |
| Two-day (8–14 credits) | $895 | $1,295 |

Pro bono attorneys and Government attorneys may attend at the member rate regardless of City Bar membership status. Law students and judicial law clerks may attend without charge subject to seat availability.

### Accreditation

The New York City Bar Association is an accredited New York CLE provider and a recognized provider for CLE credit in New Jersey, Connecticut, Pennsylvania, California, and Illinois. Credit details for each jurisdiction are available on the program registration page.

### Refund and Cancellation Policy

Registration fees are fully refundable up to 72 hours prior to program commencement. Cancellations within 72 hours of program commencement are eligible for credit toward a future program but are not eligible for refund.
